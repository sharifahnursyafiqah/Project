ABOUT HOTEL

Located close to the Putra World Trade Center, this hotel is among the top choices of corporate travelers. All the 370 rooms are spacious, beautifully decorated, and equipped with modern amenities. These also include an electronic bedside control panel, air conditioning, and attached bathrooms. A function room and banquet hall is available in case guests are interested in throwing private parties, meetings, or wedding receptions. An on-site coffee house is available for guests to relax with a cup of coffee after a hectic day, or join the gym or sauna which is equipped with all the machines guests require. Get a new makeover at the on-site salon before leaving for dinner or a visit to many interesting sites like Bukit Bintang, Petronas Twin Towers, Titiwangsa Lake Garden, and Istana Budaya - all within a short distance from the hotel.
